The Opportunity

  • Visitors walk past rare plants with no way to learn their story
  • Physical signboards are expensive, static, and don't scale
  • Kids lose interest quickly without interactive engagement
  • No digital layer to extend visitor time in the park

The Solution

  • Small QR plates placed near plants
  • Scan with any phone — no app install
  • Short audio plays about the plant's unique story
  • Available in English, Hindi, Telugu
  • Visitor stays present — eyes on nature, ears on audio

What Experium Gets

  • Premium visitor experience — world-class audio tour, like Kew Gardens or Singapore Botanic Gardens
  • Increased foot time — visitors spend longer exploring because there's more to discover at each plant
  • Kids engagement — gamification ("discover 10 plants, earn a badge") keeps families engaged longer
  • Exclusive content — personal stories about each plant: where it was sourced, cost, shipping journey — not generic internet facts
  • Passive income — monthly revenue share with zero operational effort
  • Visitor insights — which zones are popular, peak hours, engagement data
