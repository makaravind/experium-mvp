# Nature Audio Tour — Product Overview

## What It Is

A QR-code-based audio guide that lets park visitors learn about plants and nature by scanning physical plates placed throughout the park. No app install required — scan, tap, listen.

## How It Works

1. Visitor sees a small aluminum plate with a QR code near a plant
2. They scan with their phone camera → opens a mobile webpage
3. Page shows a plant photo, language selector, and "Listen" button
4. They tap listen → 60-90 second audio plays about the plant (origin, facts, significance)
5. Ads are displayed on the page (splash, banner, post-audio)

## Who It's For

- **Visitors**: Get a richer park experience without staring at their phone
- **Park (Experium)**: Premium feature that differentiates them, plus passive revenue share
- **Sponsors**: Physical brand placement on plates + digital ad impressions from a hyper-local audience
- **Us**: Platform revenue from direct-sold ads

## First Deployment

**Experium Park**, Hyderabad — 150-acre premium park with rare plant species, 500+ daily visitors at ₹1000/entry.

Target: ~50 QR plates covering rare/interesting plants across the park.

## Core Principles

- **Zero friction**: No install, no mandatory login, one tap to content
- **Audio-first**: Screen is content-light; the value is in listening while looking at the plant
- **Scans are the product**: Business model is Model B — page impressions drive revenue, audio is the hook that justifies QR codes being there
- **Indirection**: QR codes encode a permanent short ID; content is mutable server-side
- **Plates are dumb, server is smart**: No plant info printed on plates — allows reassignment without reprinting
