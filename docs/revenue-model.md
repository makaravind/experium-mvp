# Revenue Model

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Physical Marker Sponsorship (One-Time)

Sponsors pay upfront to have their logo on a marker. This covers initial manufacturing + installation costs as a goodwill arrangement.

| Item | Estimated Cost |
|------|---------------|
| Aluminum marker + printing | ₹300-800 |
| Installation (post/mounting) | ₹200-500 |
| Content creation per exhibit | ₹500-1000 |
| **Total per marker** | **₹1,000-2,300** |
| **Total for 50 markers** | **₹50,000-1,15,000** |

Sponsor pays this. No recurring fees, no performance guarantee. Pure goodwill brand placement.

### 2. Digital Ads (Recurring)

Direct-sold ads to local businesses. No ad network (volume too low for AdSense).

**Ad placements per page view:**
- Splash screen (2-3 seconds on load)
- Display banner (visible while browsing/listening)
- Post-audio interstitial

**Pricing (estimated, location-based):**

| Tier | Location | Monthly Price (est.) |
|------|----------|---------------------|
| Gold | Park entrance, main paths, high-traffic zones | ₹10,000-15,000 |
| Silver | Secondary paths, popular gardens | ₹5,000-10,000 |
| Bronze | Peripheral areas, less-visited zones | ₹3,000-5,000 |

**Target:** 3-5 direct advertisers (local restaurants, resorts, cab services, nurseries near park).

### Revenue Split

```
Phase 1: Cost Recovery
  └── 100% of ad revenue → Us (until initial investment recouped)

Phase 2: Ongoing Split
  └── TBD% → Us
  └── TBD% → Park
```

## Cost Structure

### One-Time (Covered by Sponsors)

- Marker manufacturing × 50
- Installation labor
- Content creation (expert interview + pipeline)

### Recurring (Our Costs)

| Item | Monthly Estimate |
|------|-----------------|
| Hosting (serverless + S3/CDN) | ₹500-2,000 |
| Domain | ₹100/month amortized |
| Maintainer (quarterly visit) | ₹2,000-5,000 |
| TTS generation (updates) | ₹500-1,000 |
| **Total recurring** | **₹3,000-8,000/month** |

### Revenue Projection (Conservative)

Assumptions: 500 visitors/day, 10% scan rate, 50 markers

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Daily scans | ~250 (50 markers × 5 scans avg) |
| Monthly impressions | ~7,500 |
| Monthly ad revenue (3 advertisers × ₹8K avg) | ₹24,000 |
| Monthly costs | ₹5,000 |
| **Monthly profit (pre-split)** | **₹19,000** |
| Cost recovery timeline | ~3-6 months |

*Note: These are rough estimates. Actual numbers depend on scan rate, which depends on gamification effectiveness and park marketing.*

## Key Assumptions to Validate

1. 10% scan rate is achievable with gamification + park marketing
2. Local businesses will pay ₹5-15K/month for hyper-local placement
3. Park traffic is ~500 visitors/day (verify with park)
4. Cost recovery in 3-6 months makes the deal attractive to sponsors
