# Park Partnership Brief — Experium

*A conversation starter, not a contract.*

---

## What We're Proposing

An audio guide experience for Experium Park visitors. QR code plates placed near rare and interesting plants throughout the park. Visitors scan → hear a 60-second audio narration about the plant in English, Hindi, or Telugu.

**No cost to the park. No effort from the park.**

## What Experium Gets

1. **Premium visitor experience** — world-class audio tour feature, like London's Kew Gardens or Singapore's Botanic Gardens. Aligns with "Not in London. Not in Paris. Right here in Hyderabad."

2. **Revenue share** — passive monthly income from digital ads displayed to visitors. Zero operational involvement required.

3. **Visitor engagement data** — understand which zones visitors explore most, peak hours, popular plants. Data shared with park at no cost.

4. **Marketing differentiation** — "India's first AI-powered botanical audio tour" (or similar) for PR and social media.

## What We Need From Experium

1. **Permission** to install ~50 small aluminum plates (8"×6") near select plants
2. **Access to a botanist or expert** for a one-time 3-5 hour interview about the plants
3. **Notification of landscaping changes** — if plants are moved, removed, or added
4. **Optional:** Mention in park entrance signage ("Audio Guide Available — Scan QR Codes!")
5. **Optional:** Marketing banner space about the feature within the park

## What We Handle (100%)

- All technology (app, hosting, maintenance)
- Content creation (scripts, audio, translations)
- Plate manufacturing and installation
- Physical maintenance (quarterly checks)
- Sponsor/advertiser acquisition
- All operational costs

## Revenue Model

- Initial costs covered by plate sponsors (no cost to park or us)
- Digital ad revenue: 100% retained by us until costs are recouped (est. 3-6 months)
- After recovery: revenue split (% TBD — to be discussed)
- Park receives monthly statement showing impressions and revenue

## Timeline (Estimated)

| Phase | Duration |
|-------|----------|
| Agreement + expert interview | 2-3 weeks |
| Content creation + app development | 4-6 weeks |
| Plate manufacturing | 2-3 weeks |
| Installation + testing | 1 week |
| **Total to launch** | **~10-12 weeks** |

## Risk to Experium

**None.** Zero upfront cost, zero operational effort, fully removable if it doesn't work out. Plates can be uninstalled in a day with no damage.
